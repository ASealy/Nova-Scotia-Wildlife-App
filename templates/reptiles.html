BLANDING'S TURTLE (Emydoidea blandingi)

Nesting Season - Early June - early July
No. Eggs - 10-12
Egg Pattern - Singly, in one sand/gravel beach nest

Nesting Season - Late April - May
No. Eggs - 6-40
Egg Pattern - Give birth to live young
